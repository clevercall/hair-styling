Nail Designing

Nails Designing involving fingernails and toenails are made from skin cells. structures that are made from skin cells are called skin appendages. Hairs are also skin appendages.

Manicure

Manicure is a cosmetic treatment of the hands involving shaping and often painting of the nails, removal of the cuticles, and softening of the skin. 'She never missed her weekly hairdo and manicure.

A manicure usually consists of filing and shaping the free edge of nails pushing and clipping (with a cuticle pusher and cuticle nippers) A manicure is a beauty treatment of the hands.

Pedicure

Pedicure is a cosmetic treatment of the feet and toenails. A pedicure will make sure your feet look their best. Give a pedicure to (the feet and toenails).

Pedicures include care not only for the toenails, dead skin cells are rubbed off the bottom of the feet using a rough stone. The pedicure involves soaking feet in hot water and using scalpels to remove dead skin, celluses, corns, and ingrown nails from the feet.
